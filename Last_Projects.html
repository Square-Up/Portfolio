Compound Machine

Course: POE (Principles of Engineering)

Duration: November 9, 2020 - December 4, 2020

The goal of this project was to create a compound machine with the aim of completing a simple task. In our instance, that task was stamping a piece or paper. During this project, I was in charge of my individual deliverables and the group documentation. The first thing we did was designate responsibilities and discuss how we wanted the timeline for our project to look. Once we did that, for the most part each of us worked individually on our designated parts. This project was mostly individual work, and we weren't able to meet in person, so being able to communicate with each other was key during this project.
